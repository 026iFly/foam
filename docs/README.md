# Documentation

This folder contains all important documents related to the spray foam insulation business.

## Folder Structure

### `/product-specs`
Technical specifications and datasheets for the foam products we use.

- **Closed-cell polyurethane foam A B (1).pdf** - DMJ-Spray500 specifications
  - Density: 30-55 kg/m³
  - K-Factor: ≤24 mW/(m·K)
  - Compressive Strength: ≥150 KPa
  - Closed-cell Rate: ≥90%
  - Fire resistance: Class B2

- **Open Cell polyurethane foam A B (1).pdf** - DmjSpray-501F specifications
  - Density: 8-12 kg/m³
  - K-Factor: ≤40 mW/(m·K)
  - Compressive Strength: ≥13 KPa
  - Open-cell Rate: ≥99%
  - Sound Absorption: 0.43%

### `/quotes`
Real quotes and pricing examples from suppliers and competitors.

- **Pelle Stensson Gävle 251222.pdf** - Polyterm quote with current market pricing
  - 50mm: 365 kr/m² (456 kr/m² inkl. moms)
  - 70mm: 420 kr/m² (525 kr/m² inkl. moms)
  - 100mm: 500 kr/m² (625 kr/m² inkl. moms)
  - 120mm: 550 kr/m² (687.5 kr/m² inkl. moms)
  - Setup fee: 4500 kr + moms
  - Generator: 2000 kr/day + moms
  - Extra work: 625 kr/hour + moms

### `/research`
Research, regulations, and business information for starting a spray foam business in Sweden.

- **sprayisolering_sammanfattning.pdf** - Summary of Swedish regulations and requirements
  - Required certifications (REACH diisocyanate training)
  - Fire protection requirements (13mm gypsum in bedrooms)
  - Flash-and-fill technique explanation
  - Swedish suppliers (Foam King Sweden, Ecofoam i Sverige, Polyterm)

## Using These Documents

The pricing calculator uses the data from the Polyterm quote as a baseline. You can update prices through the admin dashboard at `/admin`.

All technical specifications are used in the services page to provide accurate information to customers.
